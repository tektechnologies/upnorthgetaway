Big St. Germain Lake:

Big Saint Germain Lake is a 1622 acre lake located in Vilas County. It has a maximum depth of 42 feet.

Cozy Cottage fish include: Musky, Panfish, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Trout, Smallmouth Bass and Walleye. The lake's water is moderately clear.

Increase your chances by hitting waterways on Big St. Germain Lake. This is a trophy lake, with big fish in high numbers. Visitors have access to the lake from a public boat landing.

Town of St. Germain and surrounding area:

This charming community has been catering to vacationers and anglers for more than a century.

The paved St. Germain Bike & Hike Trail runs through the community connecting with several other area biking and nature trails.

Known best for it's fishing, St. Germain is also within a short drive of sixteen area golf courses. Winter vacationers flock here for the area's top-quality snowmobile trails as well as the Snowmobile Hall of Fame.
